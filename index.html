T-REX – Tax Reconciliation & Simple Tax Calculator

Browser-based tax tool that reconciles book income to taxable income and includes a basic calculator for estimated federal tax and effective tax rate.

  • Inputs: book income, permanent/temporary differences, NOLs, tax rate, estimated payments
  • Outputs: taxable income, estimated tax due, and a summarized reconciliation
  • Built with HTML, CSS, and vanilla JavaScript, evolving from an Excel prototype via a roadmap-style build
